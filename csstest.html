On April 26th, 2018, a nearly four year old pup, Billie, was rescued from a local Humane Society and brought home to join her new family. Ever slowly since then, with a gruff voice and a wry sense of humor, Billie has let much emerge about her past, including, but not limited to:

  • -her many years as a young pup detective;
  • -her successful chain of restaurants called Billie's and their trademark Billie-que Sauce;
  • -that she is also a doctor and a lawyer;
  • -that one day she spent as a river barge captain (with Bigfoot as her first mate);
  • -and more!

Everyday we are learning much more about Billie: qualities of great business acumen, a dark past, crime-fighting skills, great travels, and perhaps even some international dealings...
